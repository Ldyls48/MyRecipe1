Chicken Spaghetti


This recipe is one of my family's favorite dish served with a salad and breadsticks.



Ingredients

  • 3 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast, diced
  • 16 ounces package of spaghetti
  • Velveeta Cheese, 8 ounces
  • One small onion, diced
  • One green bell pepper
  • One Jalapeno pepper, diced and seeded
  • One 12 ounce can of cream of mushroom soup
  • One 16 ounce can of Chicken broth, low sodium
  • Salt, Pepper, and Cajun spice mix to taste
  • Olive Oil

Preparation


  1. Preheat Dutch oven with 2 tablespoons of Olive Oil.
  2. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and cajun season.
  3. Saute onion, bell pepper, and Jalapeno until soft.
  4. Add chicken breast cook for 10-15 minutes and remove from pan.
  5. Add chicken broth with two cups of water to Dutch Oven.
  6. Bring to boil, add salt, and spaghetti.
  7. Cook until noodles are al dente.
  8. Drain spaghetti reserving a cup of cooking liquid.
  9. Add the cream of mushroom soup with the reserved cooking liquid, until simmering.
  10. Add the cheese until melted and mixture is smooth.
  11. Add the chicken and spaghetti back to pan.
  12. Mix well.


Serve with a green salad and warm breadsticks.
